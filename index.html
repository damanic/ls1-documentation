Legacy LemonStand V1 Security Exploit

There is a malicious security exploit that a group of hackers are using to steal cardholder data. It's very important that you take steps to remedy the situation if you are still running LemonStand Version 1. You can find more information and a software patch here. Please note that we cannot install the software patch that's been provided by a third party development company.

LemonStand Version 1

Version 1 has been discontinued. LemonStand is now available as a cloud-based platform from lemonstand.com, which includes scaleable hosting and technical support in one affordable monthly price.

Important Information for v1 Customers

LemonStand v1 has been discontinued. As of Dec 31, 2014, software updates and technical support will no longer be provided by LemonStand eCommerce Inc.

Since LemonStand v1 was sold as a perpetual license, stores may continue to function beyond this date. However, it's important to note that any issues that arise such as API updates from shipping carriers or payment providers, security exploits or server compatabilties that result in a disruption to your service will not be supported beyond Dec 31, 2014.

What this means for you

As of Dec 20, 2013 we have released a cloud-based eCommerce platform which is available at lemonstand.com. We have released some documentation and tools to aid in migrating from V1 to LemonStand Cloud, as well as offering technical support and migration assistance. LemonStand is also offering large discounts to all V1 customers who wish to migrate to LemonStand Cloud.

If you have questions about your migration path, please contact us and we'll help assess your situation.

Alternatively, some customers have arranged to keep a web developer or web agency at the ready to resolve any issues that come up after Dec 31, 2014.

After 3 years of developing and supporting LemonStand v1, our move to a cloud-based platform was necessary for us to stay in business. We apologize for any inconvienence or frustration this may cause, and we will do our best to assist you wherever possible.

We thank you for your business and hope we can work together in the future.


Accessing v1 Services

All services were kept active until Dec 31, 2014. Our V1 documentation and Marketplace has also been kept online as of July 27, 2016. Please note that these services may be terminated at any time now that the software is no longer supported.
